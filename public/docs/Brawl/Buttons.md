# Buttons

```handsontable
# OPTIONS
# COLUMNS
Hex ID {
  renderer: hex
}
Dec ID
Description
# DATA
0x00 | 0  | Normal attack button (A and Z by default)
0x01 | 1  | Special attack button (B by default)
0x02 | 2  | Jump button (X, Y and Up by default)
0x03 | 3  | Shield button (L, R and Z by default)
0x04 | 4  | R, L, Z
0x05 | 5  | Smash button (C-Stick default button)
0x06 | 6  | Dpad UP
0x07 | 7  | Dpad DOWN
0x08 | 8  | Dpad RIGHT, Dpad LEFT
0x09 | 9  | Dpad LEFT
0x0A | 10 | Dpad RIGHT
0x0B | 11 | B, X, Dpad RIGHT, Dpad LEFT
0x0C | 12 | Y, X , Dpad UP, Dpad RIGHT
0x0D | 13 | B, Y, X , Dpad UP, Dpad RIGHT, Dpad LEFT
0x0E | 14 | A+B (Both pressed together)
0x0F | 15 | C-Stick (Any Direction)
0x10 | 16 | Tap jump setting. Always "held" while on and not while off.
0x11 | 17 | Unknown (Couldn't trigger this one)
0x12 | 18 | Unknown (Couldn't trigger this one)
```
